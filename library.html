Church Library

We got a wonderful church library with Christian books and other educational books. It also include a digital library with Christian movies and animation stories.

The church library is a teaching tool, important to study for the individual, the class, and the congregation. The library supports the mission of the church as well as the growth of individual members' faith. The Christian, in his study of the Bible, needs the information, discoveries, examples and distilled ideas found in books. Encourage children, youth, and adults to be informed, instructed, and encouraged in their Christian growth through the reading of good books and use of other materials, Provide our congregation with current information on what's happening in the world from a Christian perspective; and Serve our congregation in the best way possible.

In our library, there are books related to Christian stories, Educational books and Educational Guides. We also give importants to our children's academic development along with spiritual development. We have a program in which if any catechism student in our church got any doubt on any topic in his academic subjects, then he or she can approach our church librarian. The church librarian will suggest suitable books from the library or get animation videos from internet on such subjects.

LCYM Kitchen

LCYM Unit of the church is responsible for the maintenance of the church library. There are lot of expensive religions and educational books in our church library. These books have been brought to the library by the efforts of LCYM team of the church by selling home made pickles under the name 'LCYM Kitchen'.
