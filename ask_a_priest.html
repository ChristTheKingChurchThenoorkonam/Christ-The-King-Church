Christ The King Church, Thenoorkonam is situated near Vizhinjam, which was the capital of ancient 'Ayi Kingdom'. At the beginning this church was named after apostles Peter and Paul. Later it was renamed as Christ The King Church. There are few Christians in this area. Most of them are from inter religious marriage between Hindus and Christians. 24 cents of land was brought on 1966 for building this church and it was completed on 1968. During the initial days, holy mass was only performed once a month. Currently there are 38 families. Catholic associations like Catechism, LCYM, KLCA, Vincent de pole Society and Legion of Mary are active in this church. Currently there are 2 BCC units in this church (St. Mary unit and Israel unit).
