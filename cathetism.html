Cathetism Unit

Catechism heritage in Catholic church in Kerala is as old as church itself. As every church, our small church also have an effective catechism unit. We have class from nursery to degree. Catechism unit also have a separate library. Our catechism unit conduct monthly programs like 'sahityasamjam' for developing our children's talents. Every week our unit conduct a program called 'Sunday Star' in which a student will become the star of the day if he answers any question asked from the Bible. The portions (part from Bible) to study will be announced by the Sunday School principal on every preceding week. Such Sunday star will also get a small gift.
